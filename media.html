These are the films for the saga in release order with the spinoffs at the end.

A New Hope (1977)

The first movie created and initially titled just 'Star Wars', A New Hope is a tale of a princess, a farmboy, and a smuggler that need to come together in order to save the galaxy from a imposing doom.

The Empire Strikes Back (1980)

Star Wars returns in 1980 in a much darker, more dramatic way in 'The Empire Strikes Back', where consequences for previous actions must be met and new problems must be faced.

Return of The Jedi (1983)

Wrapping up the Original Trilogy, 'Return of the Jedi' brings our characters together for a final stand against the all-powerful empire and its full might.

The Phantom Menace (1999)

Going back to long before the Rebellion or Empire, 'The Phantom Menace' is about two Jedi at the height of the Jedi Order's power and what they discover that will change the galaxy forever.

Attack of The Clones (2002)

Ten years after 'Phantom', 'Attack of the Clones' reuintes our new Jedi with his love interest and political events seek to change the direction of the Jedi and Republic at hand.

Revenge of The Sith (2005)

In the thrilling conclsuion to the Prequel Trilogy, 'Revenge of the Sith' provides a story of loss and new beginnings for the galaxy, at the cost of the old ways.

The Force Awakens (2015)

In a return to the franchise, 'The Force Awakens', provides a new threat to the galaxy and to those who protect it, thirty years after 'Return'.

The Last Jedi (2017)

Immediately after the events of the previous film, 'The Last Jedi' offers a story about a struggling Resistance that ultimately finds hope in its newfound powers.

The Rise of Skywalker (2019)

The finale of the Skywalker Saga as a whole, 'Rise of Skywalker' brings back old and new together for an exciting adventure that defines the state of the galaxy.

Rogue One (2016)

The first stand-alone movie in the franchise, 'Rogue One' shows how the Rebellion managed to get their hands on the plans to the empire's superweapon and leads in to 'A New Hope'.

Solo (2018)

'Solo' is a story of a young smuggler who learns the tricks of the trade and tries to find his place in the galaxy.
