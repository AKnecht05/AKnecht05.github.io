Action Figures

Star Wars action figures have existed ever since the first movie came out in 1977. The license to make figures has been given to many different companies, including Kenner and Hasboro.

LEGO Star Wars buildable sets

LEGO Star Wars sets have been around since 1999 and are some of the most popular and collectible Star Wars items ever.

Model Ships

There are a variety of differnent small-scale models of the different space ships and vehicles of the Star Wars Universe.

Costumes

There have been many different Star Wars costumes created for both kids and adults and they often sell very well.
